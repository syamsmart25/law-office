Meet

Andretta Breaux Atkins

Personal Injury Lawyer in Plaquemine, Louisiana

Andretta Breaux Atkins is a Louisiana personal injury attorney based in Plaquemine, known for her sharp litigation skills and relentless pursuit of justice for the injured. A native of Plaquemine, Louisiana, she graduated from Southern University Law Center, earning her Juris Doctor cum laude. She represents clients across Louisiana in cases involving car, truck, and motorcycle accidents, slip-and-fall injuries, and wrongful death claims. Breaux-Atkins has earned a reputation for her hands-on approach, deep understanding of injury law, and unwavering commitment to helping clients rebuild their lives. Her practice is rooted in compassion, strategic advocacy, and a belief that every client deserves a powerful voice fighting for their recovery.
